Our Origin Story

Every art narrates a tale and every tale can create an art. The formation of art ages back to the time when we, the homo sapiens were evolved on the planet. It was the well known primitive communication method to pass on the tales since 130,000 B.C.E. It is the universal language which can be understood by any human being, irrespective of their country, continent, beliefs or whatever. But the only difference is our perceptions about it. There is a tale behind every piece of art and hence better tales create better art.

According to the ancient egyptian belief, a type of beetle (scarab beetle) symbolises immortality. Since art is immortal, here, art is being symbolised to a beetle. Once I heard someone saying, art and music are the ones for which we humans live for.

THE BEETLE TALES to me is all about this, where the fine line between the artist and the writer in me dissolves. Art and tale are correlated. Here I put out my thoughts, handmade arts, crafts and much more, & give you just a glimpse of myself. Now, grab these handcrafted goodies for yourself and your near & dear ones.
